About Me

Hi, I'm Tenzin, a Frontend web developer based in NYC. I enjoy everything from building websites from scratch to deployment, and I love building beautiful and responsive websites. I graduated from NYC College of Technology with Bachelor in Computer Science and during my semester, I took Web Development course and since then, I fell in love with it...

One of the best thing i find about Web Development is that you get to bring new ideas into a real life projects with fully functional live web sites, which is amazing! My goal is to create website contents that will satisfy viewers and customers with the information provided in a clean, beautiful and easy to navigate. I hope that the websites i will be able to work on can catch the attention of the world.

When i am not coding, I like playing video games, basketball or snowboarding upstate in the mountains during winter.
